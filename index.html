Methodology

How It Works

01 / DATA
10+ Years of Fights
Scraped directly from UFCStats — every fight since 2015. Strike stats, grappling, round-by-round, plus fighter physical attributes.
02 / MODEL
Dynamic Logit + LASSO
Discrete-choice logistic regression with L1 regularization. Leak-free rolling career stats. Style-lag state variables capture momentum.
03 / ODDS
Market Integration
Live DraftKings odds scraped Friday morning. Market implied probability feeds into the model — we look for mispriced fighters.
04 / SIZING
Kelly Criterion
Continuous bet sizing via logistic-bounded Kelly. Minimum 1 unit, scales with confidence and edge. Never recommends negative EV bets.
05 / FINISH
Finish Type Model
Separate multinomial logit predicts KO/TKO vs Submission vs Decision probability for every fight on the card.
06 / PROPS
Prop Bet Filtering
Strict threshold: edge >15pp AND confidence >0.70 AND market <0.65. Hidden by default — only shown when a real edge exists.
