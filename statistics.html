Statistical Methodology

RTP (Return to Player) — profit multiplier metric. A value of 1.20 means each unit bet returned 1.20 units (20% profit). Break-even after vig is 0.96.

One-Sample T-Test — Tests whether the model's mean RTP is statistically greater than 0.96. H₀: mean RTP = 0.96. H₁: mean RTP > 0.96. p < 0.05 indicates the edge is unlikely due to chance.

Calibration Chart — Predicted win probabilities vs actual win rates per probability bucket. Perfect calibration lies on the diagonal. Above diagonal = underconfident; below = overconfident.

Bounded Kelly Sizing — Bet sizes use a logistic-transformed Kelly fraction. Units are continuous (0–5), never reaching 5.00u. Confidence and odds structure both influence the final unit size.
